# Product Intent

## Mission

We connect board gamers with the games and people they want to play with at
conventions, turning scheduling from a pain point into an opportunity for
community.

## Desired Outcomes

- Gamers arrive at conventions with a personalized game schedule that
  maximizes their time and minimizes conflicts, so they spend their weekend
  playing games instead of searching for them.
- Hosts can plan reliably around committed player counts before the convention
  starts.
- Conventions provide reliable schedules so attendees can commit to games with
  confidence and organizers can coordinate at scale.
- First-time convention attendees feel empowered to host games because they can
  see demand and coordinate players effortlessly, lowering the barrier to
  participation.

## Anti-Goals

- Not a social network for gamers
